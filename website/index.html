About

I am a mathematics student at ETH Zurich and quantitative trader and developer at Tufalabs.

I plan to write my master's thesis in the fall semester of 2026. My mathematical interests are in pure mathematics, in particular, homogeneous dynamics and ergodic theory.

Tufalabs is a privately funded fundamental AI research lab with a trading fund focusing on deep learning based strategies. I designed and developed our trading and execution engine. I am also involved in other stages of the research process, such as reconciling backtests against our engine and building research and training infrastructure.
